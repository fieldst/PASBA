Deployed Medical Records Processing Center

Providing data for multiple PASBA applications and reports, DMRPC is the primary contact point on the management of medical documentation of military

HIMS and Coding

The Health Information Management and Coding Consultation Branch is the Army's lead agent for providing coding guidance and policy to all Military Treatment Facilities in both the deployed and fixed facilities

Data Quality

Serves as the AMEDD's executing agency for the Military Health System (MHS) Data Quality Management Control Program, providing oversight to improve the accuracy and timely submission of financial and workload data throughout the AMEDD.

Data Analysis

The mission of the Data Analysis Branch is to provide timely and useful healthcare data and expert data analysis to assist the Department of Defense, US Army, US Army Medical Command, and individual customers in providing the best healthcare for our beneficiaries.
