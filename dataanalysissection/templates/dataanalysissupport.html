The Data Analysis Branch of PASBA can assist you with the following services:

  • Requests for inpatient and ambulatory (SIDR and CAPER) patient data
  • Requests for population counts
  • Consultation on the development of M2 queries

Submit your requests for assistance to:

usarmy.jbsa.medcom-pasba.list.pasba-data-analysis@mail.mil

Note: We will email the PASBA Data Analysis requirement worksheet to you to document the requirements. You may also be required to complete a Data Use Agreement for your request.
