HIM

The Health Information Management Branch is the Army's lead agent for providing coding guidance and policy to all Medical Treatment Facilities in both the deployed and fixed facilities.

We accomplish this mission by:

  • Performing monthly coding audits on all AMEDD MTFs
  • Staffing and support of a coding help desk
  • Providing coding education and training to include ICD-10-CM/PCS
  • Development of the MHS and AMEDD medical coding and medical records policies and guidelines
  • Collaboration with the Office of the Surgeon General Clinical Consultants on policies within their field

Note: Try our electronic Coding Help Desk for the submission of all coding related questions.
