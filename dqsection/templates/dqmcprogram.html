M2 Data Quality Error Reports

The Data Quality Management Control Program (DQMCP) is a Tri-Service program to improve the overall quality of financial and clinical information for all patients of military treatment facilities.

End of Day Processing

Data Quality Managers work independently and with a data quality assurance team to develop, manage and control various data capture and data quality processes. They are responsible for submitting completed Commander's Data Quality Statement and Review List to the MTF Commander.

MTF Commanders

The MTF commanders review and approve their Commander's Data Quality Statement. They ensure monitoring mechanisms and corrective actions have been taken to improve the quality of data from their facility.

RHC Commanders

The RHCs consolidate the reports and brief the Regional Health Commanders. The MTF Commanders will attest to the status of the MTFs data quality by submitting a Quarterly Certification Statement. This statement will be forwarded to the MEDCOM DQ Manager.

Tri-Service DQM/OTSG

The DHA Management Control Program Office is responsible for conducting periodic meetings with the Service DQ Managers to review Service-level deficiencies and ensure a common understanding of the program requirements. The DHA will also ensure appropriate metrics and results from Service submissions are briefed to the Medical Business Operations Group as appropriate.
